Her Story

Rooted in Ocho Rios, Jamaica, Asia Lee is a proud Chinese-Jamaican creative whose beauty reflects both heritage and heart. Based in South Florida, she works as a model, makeup artist, and spokesmodel, bringing artistry and intention to every collaboration. Her presence is graceful, magnetic, and unmistakably her own.
